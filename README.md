## 📌 Project Overview

Customer behavior is one of the most valuable assets for retail businesses. Understanding how customers purchase products, respond to discounts, choose shipping methods, and engage with subscription programs enables organizations to make informed business decisions.

This project presents an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution that analyzes customer shopping behavior using **Python**, **PostgreSQL**, **SQL**, and **Power BI**. Starting from raw transactional data, the project performs data cleaning, exploratory analysis, SQL-based business analytics, and interactive dashboard development to uncover meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making.

The analysis is based on **3,900 customer transactions** across multiple product categories and customer demographics.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

A retail company wants to better understand customer purchasing behavior in order to improve sales performance, customer satisfaction, and long-term customer retention.

The objective of this project is to answer important business questions such as:

- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
- How do discounts influence purchasing behavior?
- Which products receive the highest customer ratings?
- Are subscription customers more valuable?
- Which shipping methods are associated with higher purchase values?
- How does revenue vary across age groups and seasons?

The insights generated from this analysis help businesses optimize marketing campaigns, customer engagement strategies, inventory planning, and operational efficiency.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Clothing products generated the highest overall revenue.
- Loyal customers contributed significantly to total business revenue.
- Customers using Express Shipping generally recorded higher purchase amounts.
- Subscription members demonstrated stronger customer engagement and repeat purchasing behavior.
- Seasonal analysis revealed relatively higher sales during the Fall season.
- Discounts positively influenced purchasing decisions while attracting high-value customers.
- Young Adult customers represented one of the most valuable customer segments.
- Customer review ratings highlighted products with consistently high customer satisfaction.
